Infamous 2 Wiki Guide

The first Infamous concluded with a dark prophecy: Electrically supercharged Cole MacGrath is the only one who can take on "The Beast" when it shows up sometime in the future. Well, the future is now, and a towering lava man, having razed Empire City, is leaving a trail of burning rubble across the nation -- and its heading to Cole's new digs in New Marais. Your task in Infamous 2 is to keep the beast off of your back. To make this easier we've provided a walkthrough for each mission, maps to powerups like Blast Shards and much more.
